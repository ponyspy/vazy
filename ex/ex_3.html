Райские вазы
Follow the Rainbow
22.12.20 — 15.01.21
Group Show Curated by TZVETNIK at ISSMAG, Moscow

Find a crockpot of butter
And eat it
Look around
Go into the woods
Go back
Dig up treasure
Ponder
Witness a bean tree growing
And a rainbow on the rear glass frame
Smile
Become upset
Grow a bean tree
And pretend like nothing happened
